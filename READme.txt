Xword: "making life easier for crossword puzzle lovers"
------------------------------------------------------
Group: Sabastian Mugazambi and Frederik Roenn Stensaeth


Static URl: thacker.mathcs.carleton.edu/cs257/mugazambis/index.py

Features implemented:
- Ability to look up words associated with a given word.
- Ability to get the definition of a word.
- Ability separate the results of the associated word look up by vocabulary category.
- Ability to narrow the search results by length of the word.
- Ability to narrow the search results by position of characters.
- Make the website responsive to the browser window size (not on our original feature list).


Have fun finding words associated to each other and completing your difficult crossword puzzle!!!

Note: Only words starting A-F are implemented due to the restrictions on the size of the database.
