Xword for Dummies

This is how you use Xword:

USAGE:

Are you stuck on a crossword puzzle and you can not find the right word associated with the given hint?

Lookup the definition of words.

SEARCH WORD:

Search for hinted word in Xword and indicate whether the word you are looking for is a noun,verb etc..

If you know the length of the word, enter it in the filter section or enter the letters you know replacing the ones you do not know with '_' eg o_r_n_e

ACCEPTED CHARECTERS:

All the search fields accept UPPER Case or lower case letters only. All other characters will be stripped off the word and the letters will compose the searched word.

In addition to letters only the letter filtering field also accepts the character '_' (underscore).
